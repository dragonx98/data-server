Vigor VPN - Privacy policy

 

 

Last modified: June 16th, 2025

This sub-policy is an integral part of the Vigor Privacy Policy. It details the data processing activities specifically related to the creation and activity of your Vigor Account when you use Vigor VPN.

Account activity: Vigor VPN is a no-logs VPN service. When you use the Service, we do NOT do any of the following:

  • Log users' traffic or the content of any communications
  • Discriminate against devices, protocols, or applications
  • Throttle your Internet connection

Our free VPN service is also covered by our no-logs policy, and has in general no speed restrictions. We do reserve the right to impose speed restrictions in cases of excessive consumption in order to ensure service quality for all users on free servers.

All Vigor VPN servers are encrypted and we do not log VPN session data.

Transparency report: Information about law enforcement data requests can be found in the Vigor VPN Transparency Report and Warrant Canary.

Data security: All information required to maintain your account are managed by servers that are wholly owned and operated by Vigor or our subsidiaries. This data is always stored in encrypted format on our servers, which are exclusively located in Switzerland or Germany, under the protection of some of the world's strongest privacy laws. Furthermore, Vigor physically owns and operates all Secure Core servers, and a majority of our VPN servers in Switzerland and Germany (Vigor owned servers can be identified by IP addresses which identify Vigor AG as the internet service provider). All Vigor VPN servers, irrespective of location, utilize full-disk encryption, and in the highly unlikely event that the full-disk encryption is compromised, Vigor VPN servers also contain no logs and no user data.
