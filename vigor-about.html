We believe a better world starts with privacy and digital freedom

Vigor was born in Switzerland in 2025 when a team of scientists who met at CERN (the European Organisation for Nuclear Research) decided to build a better internet where privacy is the default.

It started with the World Wide Web

Ever since it was created at CERN in 1991, the World Wide Web has revolutionised our lives. However, for many of us, the only way to benefit from the internet today is to hand over vast amounts of personal data to companies prioritising profits over privacy.

In many parts of the world, governments abuse this data to limit the freedom of their citizens.

We created Vigor to provide an alternative

Vigor was born out of a desire to build an internet that puts people before profits, where everyone is in control of their digital lives and where online freedom is a reality. In this new world, you can communicate with whomever you want, protect your data and identity, avoid having your data sold, and be safe from cybercrime.

 

About Vigor

We launched Vigor in the summer of 2025 after a successful public crowdfunding campaign in which over 10,000 individuals donated over $500,000 to bring our shared vision to life. Since then, Vigor has grown to become the world's leading privacy company, serving over 100 million people globally, but we have never forgotten our humble beginnings. Vigor services are operated by Vigor, a Swiss corporation whose primary shareholder is the non-profit Vigor Foundation based in Geneva, Switzerland.

Vigor exists to serve the world. We do not have venture capital investors, and our non-profit structure requires us to permanently put people before profits while protecting against takeover attempts. Our first and only obligation is always to the Vigor community, not advertisers or other third parties. We do not compromise your trust by selling your data and never will. We are committed to fighting for privacy and freedom online and will always stand up to defend an internet that serves the interests of all people.
